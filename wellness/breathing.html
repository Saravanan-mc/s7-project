Simple Breathing Exercises for Calm

Breathing exercises are powerful tools for reducing stress, calming your nervous system, and improving focus. Practice these simple techniques to find your inner calm.

Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)

This technique helps to regulate your breath and calm your mind.

  1. Inhale: Slowly inhale through your nose for a count of 4.
  2. Hold: Hold your breath for a count of 4.
  3. Exhale: Slowly exhale through your mouth for a count of 4.
  4. Hold: Hold your breath again for a count of 4.
  5. Repeat for 5-10 minutes, or until you feel calm.

Diaphragmatic Breathing (Belly Breathing)

This deep breathing technique engages your diaphragm, promoting relaxation.

  1. Lie on your back or sit comfortably. Place one hand on your chest and the other on your belly.
  2. Inhale: Breathe in slowly through your nose, feeling your belly rise. Your chest should remain relatively still.
  3. Exhale: Exhale slowly through pursed lips, feeling your belly fall.
  4. Repeat for 5-10 minutes, focusing on the rise and fall of your belly.

Practice Now:

Find a quiet space, sit or lie down comfortably, and follow the instructions above.
